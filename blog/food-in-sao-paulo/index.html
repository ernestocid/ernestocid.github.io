Places to eat in São Paulo

A list of my favorite places to eat in São Paulo.

Burguers

  • Holy Burguer: when I went, the place was tiny and always packed. I was there on holiday, showed up around lunchtime, got there half an hour before they even opened, and there were already four people in line. I tried the burger that’s ranked number 3 in the world, and yeah—it was really good. The pastrami coxinhas are also a must-try.

  • Big Kahuna Burger: they call themselves “The Temple of Bacon” and honestly, anything with bacon there is worth trying. They even serve bacon milkshakes.

Italian

  • MoMa (Modern Mamma Osteria): my favorite Italian restaurant in São Paulo, hands down. The Lasanheta de Vitelo is my go-to every single time.
